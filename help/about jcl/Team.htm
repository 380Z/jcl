Special thanks

Very special thanks goes to Robert Chandler. During development of JCL we made heavy use of his shareware application named FAR. This is an excellent tool for creating helpfiles and a must have for everyone who needs to create an HTML Help based help system. It has allowed us to rebuild the JCL helpfile from scratch several times a day without any work and well under two minutes! You can get it at the Helpware website
