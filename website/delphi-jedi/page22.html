Mailing List

If you want to keep up to date about JCL then you can join the JCL mailing list either by visiting the JCL Yahoogroups page or by sending an email to Yahoogroups (JEDI-JCL-subscribe@egroups.com). Every kind of feedback is welcome, whether it is a bugreport, suggestion, opinion, general question or request for inclusion of a particular piece of code. For bug reports and suggestions you can also use our issue tracker.

Additionally we have an announcement only list called JCL-Announce. You can join that list either by going to the JCL-Announce Yahoogroups page or by sending an email to Yahoogroups (JCL-ANNOUNCE-subscribe@egroups.com).

Before using the mailing list or the our newsgroup please have a look on our guidelines.
